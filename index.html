Hello world! I'm Sibel Eker

I'm a researcher, or scientist trying to understand complex socioeconomic systems and help decision-making with modelling and simulation tools.

Since ancient Greeks, there are two main theories about how the universe works: cosmos and chaos. To deal with complexity and uncertainty, I think, one should renounce both.

My scientific research tries to acknowledge this, and is often driven by the Shakespeare lines:

Though this be madness,
Yet, there is method in it
